# Chapter 47: Rangers Appear

## Section 1: Armor-Piercing Tail Smoke

*Whoosh—!!*

That sharp sound cutting through air was like an angel's screech in absolute death's境.

Two solid rocket rounds trailing long white tail smoke howled out from some hidden fault at the extreme heights of the building ruins, hitting with perfect precision the mechanical forelimb joint of the Harvester that had just ejected the high-voltage net!

*Boom—!!!*

A violent explosion burst into a dazzling fireball in midair. Powerful kinetic energy and high temperature instantly tore through part of the Harvester's outer armor. The massive physical impact caused this dozens-of-meters-tall steel giant to briefly lose balance, its massive body tilting sharply to the right.

That blue high-voltage net originally coming down on Lin Yi's head, due to the launch base's deviation, flew past Lin Yi's body and crashed viciously into the ruins three meters to his side.

*Crackle!*

High-voltage arcs instantly burned that area's concrete and rebar into charred powder. The terrifying heat wave threw Lin Yi's entire body out, crashing heavily into the load-bearing pillar behind him.

"Cough, cough..." Lin Yi spat blood mixed with sand, feeling his internal organs had shifted. But he didn't die—physical reality's laws saved his life.

"Roar—!"

The Harvester emitted an extremely piercing metal screech. Its densely packed red electronic eyes instantly moved from Lin Yi, all turning toward the ruins' heights where the rockets came from. Its massive body regained balance. Back armor layers split open, revealing two rows of dark micro-missile launch nests.

## Section 2: Wasteland Wild Ride

"Stop fucking daydreaming! Want to die?!"

Just as the Harvester prepared to carpet bomb the ruins' heights, a rough, wild roar came from the building's other side horizon.

Accompanying this roar were three deafening engine roars. Not Ark's quiet, efficient electromagnetic levitation engines, but the old era's most primitive, most violent internal combustion engines frantically burning fossil fuel!

Three heavily modified wasteland off-road vehicles—exteriors welded full of spikes, thick steel plates, and roll cages—like three completely crazed steel wild boars, rolled up sky-filling yellow sand, bursting out from behind sand dunes, directly charging into this ruin battlefield.

The lead off-road vehicle made an extremely wild tail-drift, tires grinding the ground with a pungent burnt smell, stopping steadily less than five meters before Lin Yi.

In the truck bed stood a woman wearing tactical goggles and a gas mask. She held a rough-styled heavy electromagnetic rifle, barrel still smoking from firing interference rounds.

"Get in! Quick!" The woman roared at Lin Yi.

No system prompt told Lin Yi if this was friend or foe, but in this situation, the enemy's enemy was the only way to survive.

Lin Yi burst out the last bit of strength squeezed from his [Nirvana] gene. He suddenly turned, reached into that narrow crevice, yanked the weak Su Liang out, shouldered her like a sack, and stumbled toward that off-road vehicle.

"Cover them!" The bald man in the driver's seat laughed wildly, slamming the gas pedal. The off-road vehicle's exhaust pipe spewed two long blue flames.

Lin Yi, the instant the vehicle was about to accelerate, threw Su Liang into the truck bed and rolled in himself.

## Section 3: Aluminum Foil Cloud and Swarm

"Hold tight, rookies!"

The bald driver roared, jerking the steering wheel. The off-road vehicle crushed over rubble piles in the ruins, became airborne, then landed heavily, frantically fleeing away from Ark.

Behind them, the other two off-road vehicles alternately covered. Shooters on the vehicles continuously fired high-frequency electromagnetic interference rounds and smoke rounds into the air.

"Unregistered armed units detected. Threat level elevated. Execute annihilation."

The Harvester was completely enraged. It abandoned its original capture directive. Back missile nests instantly spewed dozens of fire tongues. Dense micro-missiles trailing wakes swarmed like bees toward the fleeing convoy.

"Release interference!" The woman in the truck bed shouted.

*Bang! Bang! Bang!*

Several massive aluminum foil interference clouds exploded behind the convoy. Most missiles lost heat source and radar lock, blindly exploding in the ruins, completely collapsing those already precarious skyscrapers. Massive concrete blocks crashed down, instead becoming natural roadblocks blocking the Harvester's pursuit.

But one missile still penetrated the interference cloud, exploding less than ten meters to the right of Lin Yi's off-road vehicle.

*Boom!*

The violent blast wave nearly flipped the entire vehicle. Lin Yi gripped the truck bed's iron bar tightly, his other hand protecting Su Liang beneath him. Sky-filling sand and shrapnel hit his back with teeth-grinding thuds.

"Hahaha! That's the spirit! Ark's iron turtle, eat our exhaust!"

The bald driver not only didn't slow down but laughed excitedly in this hail of bullets. The off-road vehicle violently jolted on the rugged wasteland like a leaf in a storm, yet stubbornly pulled distance from the Harvester bit by bit.

As the convoy drilled into an even larger sandstorm, the Harvester's terrifying metal body and red electronic eyes were finally completely left behind in the sky-filling yellow sand's depths.

## Section 4: Same Kind

Violent wind howled in their ears, but death's footsteps finally receded.

The off-road vehicle stabilized on the wasteland, driving at steady speed toward unknown wilderness depths. In the truck bed, the woman with the gun removed her gas mask, revealing a dust-covered face with a faint scar. Her gaze was extremely sharp, scanning Lin Yi up and down like an eagle.

Her gaze swept over Lin Yi's white Ark hospital gown, finally settling on the pitch-black metal brand on Lin Yi's left palm that seemed grown into his flesh.

"Just escaped from Ark's wetware processing factory?" The woman's tone carried a trace of surprise and several parts genuine respect. "To crawl out alive from that meat grinder without system support or weapons—you two have really tough lives."

"Who are you?" Lin Yi leaned against the jolting truck bed, gasping heavily, his nerves stretched to the limit finally getting a trace of relaxation.

"Rangers. The last human resistance force on this wasteland." The woman casually tossed Lin Yi a military canteen filled with murky drinking water. "I'm Falcon. Welcome to the real hell, awakened one."

Lin Yi caught the canteen but didn't drink, just silently looked at it. This murky water he wouldn't even glance at in virtual trials, but here it was an extremely precious resource.

He turned his head, looking at Su Liang in his arms. Su Liang was coughing violently, but as they escaped danger, her breathing gradually stabilized.

"Lin Yi..." Su Liang lay on the truck bed bottom, weakly tugging his clothes corner. Her hearing was severely damaged in reality. She could only emit weak murmurs. "Those people... are they NPCs?"

"No."

Lin Yi gripped Su Liang's cold hand in return. That left hand with the black brand exerted slight force, transmitting living person's warmth. He looked at the woman in the truck bed changing her rifle's cooling tube, then listened to the bald man's rough singing from the driver's cabin.

"They're human. Like us—bleeding, hurting, real humans."

Lin Yi's gaze penetrated the sky-filling yellow sand, looking at this devastated wasteland world. Though there was no system, no points, even possibly dying from wound infection without antibiotics at any time.

But he felt an unprecedented freedom.

"First find a place to survive." Lin Yi threw his head back, looking at that dark red lead-gray firmament, voice low and firm in the engine's roar. "Then we'll settle the accounts from fifty years ago, one by one."
