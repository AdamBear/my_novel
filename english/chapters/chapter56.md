# Chapter 56: Purge Directive

## Section 1: Falling Death God

BOOM—!!!

The dull explosion seemed to detonate directly in the brain nerves. The concrete and rock layer over ten meters thick collapsed with a roar under the power of bunker-buster bombs. Large amounts of rubble, rebar, and choking dust crashed into the armory like a waterfall, instantly drowning out the originally faint light.

The pungent smell of gunpowder mixed with the wasteland's characteristic radioactive dust, madly pouring in.

"Cough cough!"

Lin Yi agilely pulled Su Liang and rolled behind several heavy iron boxes filled with ammunition the instant of the explosion. Rubble crashed onto the iron boxes, making heart-stopping loud noises.

In the darkness, several ghostly blue plasma tail flames jetted in from the huge breach overhead.

They were four pitch-black mechanical hounds, each the size of an adult male lion. They stepped on the collapsed ruins, their alloy claws at the ends of their limbs deeply embedded in the concrete floor.

[Warning...]

No system warning.

Nothing appeared on Lin Yi's retina. But the black metal brand on his left palm, like a red-hot branding iron, transmitted a more precise sense of deadly crisis than any system alert.

"Unregistered targets detected. Confirmed as high-risk awakened ones. Execute maximum purge directive."

The mechanical hounds' abdominal speakers emitted emotionless electronic synthesized voices. The next second, the dense crimson electronic eyes on their heads suddenly lit up in the darkness, four dazzling red laser scanning lines crisscrossing toward the cover where Lin Yi was hiding.

"Didn't the system teach you not to talk before shooting?"

Lin Yi sneered coldly. He didn't wait for them to fire, but instead kicked off with both legs, using the inhuman explosive power granted by the [Nirvana] gene to shoot out from the side of the cover like a true lone wolf!

The old-era AK assault rifle in his hand instantly raised. He didn't need to aim with his eyes—muscle memory and instinct had already locked onto the target.

Bang bang bang bang bang!

In this real world without any pain blocking or health bar indicators, powder weapons erupted with the most primitive roar. 7.62mm steel-core armor-piercing rounds, carrying dazzling muzzle flash, viciously tore through the darkness.

The massive recoil slammed into Lin Yi's shoulder, but his gun-holding arm remained steady as a mountain.

The head sensor of the frontmost mechanical hound was instantly blown apart, sparks flying. But it didn't "stagger" like monsters in games. Instead, relying on mechanical instinct, it opened its metal maw filled with high-frequency cutting teeth and lunged at Lin Yi through the hail of bullets!

## Section 2: Waltz in the Darkness

"Three o'clock direction! Distance four meters, pouncing!"

Amid the deafening gunfire, Su Liang's extremely penetrating cool voice came from behind.

In this underground space destroyed by EMP, filled with gunsmoke and without lighting, Su Liang's system-enhanced ears once again became the most terrifying radar on the battlefield. She couldn't hear heartbeats (because the opponents were machines), but she could clearly hear the sound of the hounds' limb hydraulic pumps charging, the hum of high-frequency gears rotating.

Lin Yi didn't look back at all, didn't use his eyes to confirm. He had absolute, pathologically trusting faith in Su Liang's callouts based on their life-and-death complicity.

The instant Su Liang shouted "pouncing," Lin Yi suddenly slid.

Whoosh—!

The mechanical body reeking of heavy machine oil flew over Lin Yi's scalp, claws even severing a strand of his hair.

And the instant the hound missed, Lin Yi's gun muzzle had already smoothly raised upward.

Bang bang bang!

Three bullets precisely shot from the lower jaw into the mechanical hound's most vulnerable neck neural center. Black insulating oil spurted out like blood. The mechanical hound convulsed violently in midair, then crashed heavily to the ground, becoming a pile of scrap metal.

But the crisis wasn't resolved—the other three hounds had already flanked from both sides.

One of them silently circled to Lin Yi's visual blind spot, less than two meters away!

Swish!

A gray-black figure flashed out from behind cover like a ghost.

It was Su Liang.

She had no gun, but her assassin instinct honed through countless trials remained deadly under real gravity. Using the momentum of her charge, her body flexibly twisted, avoiding the hound's sweeping claws. The military tactical knife in her hand, carrying a desolate cold gleam, extremely precisely stabbed into the armor gap at the hound's front limb shoulder blade.

That was exactly where the servo motor hydraulic line was located!

"Break for me!" Su Liang gritted her teeth, wrist forcefully twisting.

Rip!

The thick hydraulic line was severed, high-pressure gas and oil spraying out. The hound's right front limb instantly lost power, its massive body pitching forward.

Lin Yi didn't even turn his head. He even predicted where Su Liang would knock down the hound, backhandedly drawing the P-19 large-caliber kinetic pistol from his outer thigh holster and firing one shot at that mechanical head falling to the ground.

BOOM!

The hound's head exploded like a watermelon.

The two stood back-to-back in the darkness, one holding a scalding assault rifle, one reverse-gripping an oil-dripping knife. No system party panel, but their coordination was like two killing machines sharing one brain.

## Section 3: Collapsed Defense Line

"Fuck! Suppressing fire!"

The Ranger boss's rough roar came from outside the armory.

Immediately after, heavy machine gun roars and high-frequency electromagnetic pulse blue light illuminated the passage. Falcon charged in carrying a heavy electromagnetic rifle, turning the last two mechanical hounds trying to pounce on Lin Yi into sieves.

"Lin Yi! Su Liang! Not dead, right?!" Falcon shouted. Her gas mask was covered with dust and others' blood.

"Not yet." Lin Yi loaded a new magazine, pulled the bolt. Hot shell casings fell to the ground with crisp sounds. "This is just the vanguard."

"It's chaos up there!"

The boss strode in dragging his mechanical left arm, his face full of gunsmoke traces, single eye showing unprecedented gravity. "Ark's iron turtles have surrounded the entire Ash Canyon! There are at least three Harvesters up there, plus dozens of mechanical hounds! We're exposed!"

"This camouflage net is lead-grade, how did they find us?" Su Liang frowned.

"It's a tracking beacon."

Lin Yi looked coldly at the pools of black machine oil on the ground, extending his left hand and pointing at the pitch-black brand on his palm. "We made such a big commotion in the processing factory. Though Ark's system crashed, the physical mainframe must have marked a high-frequency tracking signal on my genes or this fragment."

The air instantly quieted.

Falcon's gun muzzle instinctively shifted half an inch, looking at Lin Yi with extremely complex eyes. This meant Lin Yi and Su Liang were the living bait that brought this catastrophe.

"So what?" Lin Yi showed no fear. He looked directly into the Ranger boss's eyes. "Do you want to hand me over in exchange for Ark's mercy?"

The boss stared intently at Lin Yi. After several seconds, he suddenly spat bloody saliva.

"Mercy my ass. On the wasteland, Ark never leaves survivors." The boss raised his mechanical arm. "The people I saved are my people! But this camp can't be held—we must evacuate immediately!"

## Section 4: Offense and Defense Reversed

"Evacuate?"

Lin Yi looked at the huge breach overhead still continuously dropping rubble, then listened to the waves of despairing tread-crushing sounds and explosions from above.

Those were dozens of lives being slaughtered by Ark's machines.

"In this sea of sand without even cover, where can you evacuate with a group of wounded? Once your vehicles are locked by Harvester missiles, everyone will become charcoal on the wasteland."

Lin Yi's voice wasn't loud, but amid the rumbling explosions it carried a bone-chilling calm.

"What do you mean?" The boss frowned.

"The meaning is simple."

Lin Yi turned his head, looking toward Ark One's direction. In those eyes that had crossed half a century and witnessed countless deaths and betrayals, an extremely mad fire of rebellion was burning.

"You just said Ark sent all its forces to surround this place, right?"

Lin Yi's lips slowly curved into a bloodthirsty arc.

"This means right now, that turtle shell's physical exhaust port has unprecedentedly empty defenses."

The boss and Falcon simultaneously froze. They looked at this young man wearing a tattered hospital gown over a tactical vest like looking at a madman.

"Running will only get us killed on the road."

Lin Yi holstered the P-19 pistol back in his thigh holster, both hands leveling the scratch-covered AK rifle, eyes cold as if looking at a group of already-dead machines.

"Detonate this armory, bury those iron dogs above."

"Then, we don't run."

"We're going to blow Ark's mainframe sky-high."
