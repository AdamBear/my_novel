# Chapter 72: Physical Persuasion

## Section 1: Gunfire and Tin

"Fire!!!"

Accompanied by the mercenary's hoarse roar, Black Water Bar instantly became a bullet-riddled inferno.

Over a dozen assault rifles and submachine guns simultaneously spat orange fire tongues. Dense bullets wove into a death net, instantly tearing through the dim air, completely covering the booth area where Lin Yi stood.

But zero point one seconds before the guns fired, Lin Yi moved.

He didn't make any tactical evasive roll, but instead suddenly gripped the edges of that heavy tin table with a deep crater smashed into it.

The [Nirvana] second stage's terrifying explosive power detonated in muscle fibers. Lin Yi roared lowly, actually forcibly uprooting this two-hundred-plus-pound solid iron table welded to the floor, holding it like a massive tower shield in front of himself and the Ranger boss!

Ding ding clang—!!!

Bullets poured down like a rainstorm on the iron table, sparks flying everywhere, the harsh metallic twisting sounds making eardrums ache. Large-caliber stray bullets grazed the table edge, shattering the bar cabinet behind into pieces, inferior alcohol mixed with glass shards raining down.

"What the fuck kind of monster strength is this!"

The boss hiding behind the iron table gasped. Even his mechanical left arm might not be able to instantly uproot this welded iron pillar, yet this seemingly lean young man did it with pure physical strength.

"Cover me!"

Lin Yi didn't give enemies a chance to reload. He propped up the riddled iron table with one hand, right hand drawing the P-19 kinetic pistol, exposing half his body from the table's side.

No system crosshairs, purely relying on killing instinct honed through countless life-and-death edges.

Bang! Bang! Bang!

Electromagnetically accelerated armor-piercing rounds screamed out with lethal kinetic energy.

Three gunshots, three mercenaries charging closest had their foreheads explode in blood mist. Without even screaming, they collapsed like puppets with cut strings. Their bulletproof helmets were like fragile paper shells before the P-19.

## Section 2: Flesh Meat Grinder

"He only has one pistol! Press forward! Use shotguns to blast him!" Mad Dog clutched his shattered nose, face covered in blood, hiding behind the bar counter screaming madly.

Two burly mercenaries holding large-caliber shotguns tried to flank from left and right.

But they clearly underestimated the "Wasteland Tyrant's" close-combat oppression.

Lin Yi suddenly kicked away that riddled iron table. The two-hundred-pound iron table, carrying howling wind sounds, viciously smashed toward the left mercenary like an out-of-control truck, directly crushing him against the wall with his gun, all ribs shattered.

Simultaneously, Lin Yi himself like a black lightning bolt, braving the right mercenary's shotgun fire, zigzagged in a ground-hugging sprint!

Boom!

A shotgun blast grazed Lin Yi's shoulder, hundreds of steel pellets punching a honeycomb into the wall. If it were an ordinary carbon-based organism, even being grazed would tear off a large chunk of flesh.

But the invisible dense membrane on Lin Yi's body surface and bone density surpassing titanium alloy forcibly withstood several edge shrapnel scrapes. He only felt his shoulder slightly numb, speed not decreasing at all, instantly closing in on that mercenary.

"Die!" The mercenary tried in terror to pump the slide for a second chambering.

Crack.

Lin Yi's left hand bearing the black brand shot out like a viper, grabbing the scalding shotgun barrel, violently bending it upward. The high-strength barrel was actually bent ninety degrees by his bare hands!

Immediately after, Lin Yi smoothly seized a tactical kukri from the opponent's waist, backhanding a sharp diagonal slash.

Squelch—!

Warm blood sprayed fountain-like onto Black Water Bar's moldy ceiling.

From battle start to now, only thirty seconds had passed. The entire bar floor already had seven or eight corpses lying on it. The intense bloody smell completely overwhelmed the alcohol and inferior tobacco odors.

## Section 3: Unreasonable Rules

The remaining seven or eight mercenaries were completely terrified.

They were gangsters, only daring to bully the weak while fearing the strong, collecting protection fees by relying on numbers. Facing this ultimate killing machine that charged through bullets, dismantled guns bare-handed, killed without blinking, and had no excess movements, their psychological defenses instantly collapsed.

"Monster... this guy's a monster! Run!"

Someone threw down their gun, scrambling toward the bar's back door.

Lin Yi didn't chase. He casually pinned that blood-dripping kukri into a nearby wooden pillar, stepping on shell casings and blood water covering the floor, step by step walking toward deputy commander "Mad Dog" hiding behind the bar counter.

The Ranger boss followed behind holding his revolver. He originally came to help, but found he only fired one shot the entire time. This wasn't some black market shootout—this was a one-sided, pure physical dimensional reduction strike.

"You... don't come closer!"

Mad Dog no longer had his previous arrogance. He sat paralyzed behind the bar counter. Though holding a gun, his hands shook too much to aim. Looking at Lin Yi's pitch-black eyes showing no emotional fluctuation, he seemed to see death beckoning.

"Boss Lin! Big Brother Lin! Let's talk this out!"

Mad Dog shouted in breakdown. "I understand the rules! This batch of goods I'll give for free! I'll refund the protection fee!"

Lin Yi walked to the bar counter, looking down at him from above.

"On my turf, there are no such underworld rules."

Lin Yi raised his right foot, the heavy combat boot sole viciously stepping on Mad Dog's intact right leg knee, then suddenly exerting force and grinding!

Crack—!!!

"Ahhhhhhhhh—!!!"

Mad Dog's kneecap was directly crushed into powder by this hydraulic-press-like terrifying force. The shrill scream even penetrated the bar's soundproof walls, echoing through Black Rock City's mid-level streets.

"I just asked you, where are the antibiotics and micron-level nerve suturing equipment?" Lin Yi's voice was flat as asking about today's weather. "My patience is very limited. She's still waiting for me to return for surgery."

## Section 4: Seizing the Nest

"In the vault! In the VIP booth in back! Password is 147258!"

Before extreme physical pain, Mad Dog stripped out Iron Blood Mercenary Corps' underwear, vomiting blood while wailing. "That was goods we planned to send to upper level big shots... all yours! Everything's yours!"

Lin Yi removed his foot.

He turned to look at the Ranger boss. "Boss, go collect the goods."

The boss swallowed, immediately stepping over corpses toward the VIP booth behind the bar. Less than two minutes later, he emerged not only carrying a silver sterile medical case emitting cold air, but also shouldering two heavy military supply bags.

"Fuck, these bastards really are rich." The boss's single eye flashed with excited light. "Inside there's not only the highest-grade broad-spectrum antibiotics and the bionic nerve surgery device you wanted, but also two high-frequency vibration knives and several crates of military-grade armor-piercing rounds! All unopened good stuff!"

"Very good."

Lin Yi nodded with satisfaction. He turned, looking at the surrounding few remaining mercenaries trembling in fear, hands on heads squatting in the corner.

Lin Yi pulled out from his pocket that [Ark Spider Mech CPU] stained with Mad Dog's nosebleed, casually tossing it on the bar counter.

"Go back and tell your Iron Blood Mercenary Corps commander."

Lin Yi picked up a clean cloth, wiping blood off his hands, eyes contemptuous. "This chip not only bought your surgical equipment, but also conveniently bought this outpost."

"From today, Black Water Bar and that platform outside belong to me, Lin Yi."

"Anyone who's not convinced can bring people anytime to twist my head off."

Lin Yi casually tossed the blood-wiping cloth onto Mad Dog's twitching face, striding out the bar's entrance.

"Boss," Lin Yi said without turning back to the Ranger leader behind him, "signal Falcon. Have her bring all three hundred of our brothers here."

"This Black Rock City should change owners."
