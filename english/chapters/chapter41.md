# Chapter 41: 450 Points of Bad Debt

## Section 1: Silent Abyss

The white pixel storm was like countless extremely fine bone-scraping steel knives, mercilessly stripping away the last traces of existence in this space.

Lin Yi's body disintegration had reached seventy percent. His right leg and right arm had completely turned into drifting green code, and his left half had become semi-transparent. In this bottom-layer physical formatting, pain nerves were completely erased, replaced by a maddening sense of nothingness—you could clearly see your body being disassembled, but your brain couldn't receive any feedback.

Su Liang nearby was in worse condition.

She curled up on the constantly virtualizing metal grid, both hands covering her ears tightly. Blood mixed with green pixels continuously poured from her seven orifices. The sky-filling high-frequency formatting white noise was, for her extremely sensitive hearing, no different from a nuclear test detonating in her brain matter.

She couldn't hear anymore.

Her proud "radar" was completely destroyed before this dimensional reduction strike symbolizing absolute rationality. She was like a drowning person thrown into the deep sea's blind zone, surrounded by suffocating darkness and silence. This was the first time since entering the trials that Su Liang felt so completely powerless.

## Section 2: Soul Connection

【Formatting Progress: 85%... Preparing to clear cache.】

The cold system countdown flickered on their retinas. They had only a dozen seconds left.

Lin Yi gritted his teeth, using his remaining left arm to support himself on the ground, dragging his broken half-body like a dying reptile, inching bit by bit toward Su Liang.

He couldn't just die like this. At least he couldn't let her dissipate alone in this absolute silence.

He crawled to Su Liang's side, looking at her blood-stained face, pale from fear and despair. She had her eyes closed, her body convulsing unconsciously.

Lin Yi extended his left hand covered with blue veins, gripping the [Fragment of Reality] tightly, and gently pressed it against Su Liang's forehead.

"Su Liang."

Lin Yi silently called out in his mind while reversely activating the [Pain Network] he had just acquired.

Since physical hearing had been stripped away, he would use soul vibrations to transmit sound!

*Buzz—*

The moment the fragment touched Su Liang's skin, a weak but extremely tenacious mental link penetrated the sky-filling white noise, establishing directly in the depths of their consciousness.

Su Liang's eyes suddenly opened.

In this noisy silence, she actually "heard" Lin Yi's hoarse, exhausted yet slightly warm voice in her mind.

"Lin Yi..." Su Liang's lips moved slightly, spitting out blood mixed with pixel fragments. She looked at this man before her with only half his body remaining, a trace of extremely complex emotion flashing in her eyes.

## Section 3: Settlement

"Cough..." Lin Yi looked at her, his lips pulling into an extremely difficult bitter smile, transmitting his voice through the mental link. "Looks like you still can't repay the debt."

In this dying tranquility, they didn't cry, didn't pray, and had no great enlightenment or repentance.

Su Liang looked at him, those originally cool eyes now carrying intense unwillingness and fierceness.

"Principal 300 points, plus 50% compound interest for one instance..." Su Liang responded through gritted teeth in her mind. "Lin Yi, I owe you exactly 450 points."

Her gaze passed over Lin Yi's shoulder, as if seeing a scene from long ago.

"In that elevator in 'Lightless Hospital,' you shouldn't have pressed the door open button to pull me up." Su Liang's voice carried a desolate mockery. "If you had thrown me down to feed that blind doctor, or directly killed me in the fake human apartment, you might have already cleared with high scores and be drinking synthetic coffee in the transit station."

Lin Yi looked at her and shook his head.

"I never take losses in business."

Lin Yi's consciousness fluctuation was abnormally calm, as if conducting a cold business negotiation. "I said you're my most valuable asset. What kind of logic is there in casually throwing away assets?"

## Section 4: Ultimate Accomplices

"Then you've taken a huge loss this time, Boss Lin."

Su Liang let out a miserable laugh, a real, non-pixelated tear sliding from the corner of her eye. "Even lost your life, got nothing in return."

"Yeah, huge loss." Lin Yi lowered his eyelids.

In the final seconds of life, two desperados who held "profit maximization" as their creed were using the coldest debt and calculation to cover the softest things deep in their hearts.

They didn't say "I love you," nor did they say "I'm sorry." In this man-eating Infinite Trial, emotions were poison that could kill.

But that bad debt called "450 points" became their unique, back-to-back soul bond in this hell.

"This bad debt..." Su Liang looked at Lin Yi, her eyes gradually becoming fervent and resolute. "I won't let it go just like this. Lin Yi, even if we go to hell, I'll find you to collect it. Greed is the strongest chain between us."

"I'll be waiting." Lin Yi closed his eyes.

【Formatting Progress: 95%... Executing final erasure.】

Just as they prepared to calmly face complete nothingness, the faint "pulsation" in Lin Yi's left palm transmitted clearly into Su Liang's brain through the [Pain Network] link.

*Thump. Thump.*

Su Liang suddenly froze.

Although her hearing was destroyed by white noise, although Lin Yi couldn't locate the physical exhaust port's coordinates in the storm, in this moment of shared pain, Su Liang truly "felt" that vibration frequency from the physical mainframe in reality through Lin Yi's hand.

In absolute despair, this faint pulsation was like lightning splitting through darkness, instantly lighting up Su Liang's dying eyes.
