# Chapter 66: Fading System Panel

## Section 1: Phantom Syndrome

Six hours after leaving Ark One ruins.

The wasteland temperature began plummeting cliff-like as the dark red sun set westward. From the fifty-degree midday heat that could roast skin, it rapidly approached freezing point.

Four hundred ragged awakened remnant forces, like an extremely exhausted gray serpent, dragged heavy steps following behind the Rangers' roaring off-road vehicle. The line continuously transmitted suppressed coughing and heavy breathing. At intervals, someone would collapse face-first into the scorching yet rapidly cooling gravel from exhausted stamina or radiation infection, never to rise again.

No one helped them up.

After experiencing that bloody selection at the ruins entrance, this group of "frozen meat" had deeply comprehended the wasteland's first law—compassion consumes calories.

Lin Yi sat on the bouncing vehicle roof, one leg bent, playing with a 7.62mm brass bullet in his hand.

Occasionally he would unconsciously raise his right hand, making a gesture in midair.

Nothing happened.

"Phantom syndrome."

Falcon, sitting in the truck bed cleaning his rifle, saw his movement and exhaled a smoke ring, voice somewhat distorted in the engine roar. "Every awakened one who just escaped the greenhouse has this problem. Always thinking there's still a panel in front of them, always thinking pressing the backpack button will conjure bullets and health potions from thin air."

"Being accustomed to data quantification, suddenly returning to reality is indeed somewhat uncomfortable." Lin Yi didn't deny it, looking at his hands covered with calluses and fine scars.

Without the intuitive display of [Stamina: 100/100], he could only assess his physical limits through muscle soreness; without the [Backpack] function, the few spare magazines and half canteen of water hanging on him were his entire life savings.

In this real world, once bullets ran out, a gun was just a fire poker; once a major artery was severed, without "instant hemostatic gel" from the points shop, a person would go into hemorrhagic shock within three minutes.

Extremely low error tolerance—this was the most despairing part.

## Section 2: Flesh Engine

"However, some things aren't false."

Lin Yi closed his eyes, feeling that scalding heat flow surging with each breath inside his body.

[Nirvana] gene second stage.

After completely breaking free from the system's suppression, this genetic potential forced out through countless life-and-death trials finally revealed its true terror in this carbon-based flesh.

Lin Yi could clearly feel his bone density was several times higher than ordinary people, as if titanium alloy had been injected into his marrow. His metabolism became extremely slow yet efficient, stomach acid concentration sufficient to digest raw meat carrying trace radiation, while his liver frantically filtered toxins from the air.

If those survivors behind were old bicycles that could break down anytime, he was now a nuclear-powered engine that didn't need plugging in.

This was the "perfect biological weapon" Ark had schemed to obtain.

"Cough cough..."

From the truck bed bottom, Su Liang let out a weak cough. She was wrapped in a tattered military coat given by the Rangers, face still pale, but eyes had regained clarity.

"How's the radar recovery?" Lin Yi looked down at her.

"The damaged nerves still hurt. But barely usable." Su Liang closed her eyes, right ear pressed against the bouncing truck bed. "Without the system's shielding restrictions, the sounds on this wasteland... are too chaotic. Wind howling, crustal movement, even some massive creature's heartbeat dozens of kilometers away."

"Then filter out those useless noises."

Lin Yi said flatly. "You're not the system's scanner anymore. You're your own master. Learn to control it, not be controlled by it."

Su Liang didn't refute, only furrowing her brow slightly, seemingly trying hard to adjust her auditory nerve reception frequency.

## Section 3: Tremors Beneath the Sand Sea

"Boss, at this speed we can't reach the next shelter before dark."

In the driver's cabin, the bald driver loudly reported the warning to the Ranger leader in the passenger seat. "Those burdens behind are walking too slowly. Plus the blood smell and human scent are too strong—in this sand sea, that's living bait!"

"Tell that Lin guy, if they keep dawdling, we'll floor it and ditch them!" The boss's mechanical left arm emitted irritable hydraulic sounds.

Just as he finished speaking.

Su Liang, who had been resting with closed eyes, suddenly snapped them open.

"Stop the vehicle!" Her voice was hoarse yet carried an undeniable sharpness. "Stop immediately!"

The bald driver instinctively slammed the brakes.

Screech—!

The off-road vehicle slid over ten meters on the sand, stopping abruptly. The hundreds of survivors following behind were caught off guard, crashing into each other like dominoes, erupting in chaotic cursing.

"What's happening?" Lin Yi instantly flipped down from the roof, P-19 kinetic pistol already chambered in his hand.

"Underground..."

Su Liang's face became extremely ugly in an instant. She stared fixedly at a stretch of undulating dunes to the convoy's left. "Something's moving at high speed below. The sound of friction against sand grains is very dense... not a machine, it's alive! Very large, over five meters!"

"It's a wasteland native aberration!" Falcon's face changed, immediately raising his heavy electromagnetic rifle.

The nuclear cleansing and unknown spore contamination fifty years ago not only destroyed human civilization but completely altered Earth's food chain. Those beasts that survived under intense radiation mutated into bloodthirsty monsters more terrifying than machines.

"It's going for those survivors!" Su Liang shouted.

## Section 4: Settlement of Hard Currency

Boom!

Accompanied by a dull roar, a sand dune thirty meters from the convoy suddenly exploded.

Through flying yellow sand, a terrifying behemoth comparable in size to an old-era armored vehicle burst from the ground! It was a mutated desert monitor lizard, but its back was covered with bony spikes, skin showing a sickly dark red from radiation exposure, massive jaws dripping corrosive mucus.

"Ahhhhh—!!!"

Several survivors at the formation's edge hadn't even reacted before being instantly coiled by the lizard's several-meter-long tongue covered in barbs, dragged directly into the abyssal maw.

Crack! Crack!

Hair-raising bone-crushing sounds echoed across the wasteland.

The crowd instantly collapsed. Screams and cries merged into chaos, the just-established order crumbling like paper before absolute terror.

"Fire!" The Ranger boss roared, single-handedly raising the heavy machine gun to strafe.

But ordinary kinetic bullets hitting the lizard's thick keratinous scales only sparked, completely unable to penetrate its defense. The lizard, enraged by gunfire, swung its massive tail, directly sweeping over a dozen fleeing survivors into midair.

"Too hard! Unless we use armor-piercing explosive rounds!" Falcon gritted his teeth.

"Not necessary."

A cold voice rang out amid the engine roar.

Lin Yi didn't fire. He holstered his pistol, backhand drawing the high-frequency vibration tactical knife seized from the processing factory.

Under everyone's horrified gaze, not only did he not retreat seeking cover, but his legs suddenly kicked off the ground, shooting straight toward the rampaging mutant lizard like a cannonball!

"Is he insane?! That's a C-class aberration!" The boss's single eye widened.

Lin Yi wasn't insane.

The invisible dense membrane catalyzed by the [Nirvana] gene on his body surface withstood the high-intensity radiation emanating from the lizard at this moment.

The instant he was less than two meters from the lizard, its foul-smelling maw suddenly snapped at him.

"Beast."

On Lin Yi's left palm, that black [Fragment of Reality] brand erupted with dazzling red light. He didn't dodge, but formed his left hand into a fist, meeting the lizard's jaw that could bite through steel beams, viciously punching upward!

Bang—!!!

A deafening muffled sound.

The terrifying explosive power of gene second stage, combined with the physical fragment's destructive properties, manifested fully in this punch.

The lizard's massive head was actually knocked forcibly to one side by this punch, over a dozen fangs shattered, dark green blood spraying wildly!

"Die!"

Lin Yi borrowed the punch's recoil, body leaping into the air, jumping directly onto the lizard's back. He gripped the high-frequency vibration knife in reverse with both hands, aiming at a gap in the lizard's neck scales, using all his strength to stab down viciously!

Zzzt—!

The high-frequency cutting buzz mixed with the monster's scream echoed across the wasteland. Lin Yi didn't withdraw the blade, but gripped the handle with both hands, dragging it down along the lizard's spine!

A terrifying two-meter-long gash was instantly cut open.

The lizard's massive body convulsed violently several times, finally collapsing thunderously, kicking up sand clouds.

Complete silence.

The Rangers stared dumbfounded at Lin Yi standing on the lizard corpse, bathed in blood. Without mech armor, without heavy firepower, relying purely on physical strength and a knife, he killed a C-class aberration in under a minute!

Lin Yi pulled out the knife covered in green blood, wiping it on the lizard's scales.

He looked down from his height at the hundreds of trembling survivors around, too scared to even cry.

"What are you looking at?"

Lin Yi's eyes were cold as an asura. He kicked aside a chunk of lizard flesh.

"This is tonight's dinner. Those who want to live, come cut meat yourselves. Those who don't want to eat this radioactive raw meat, go dig a hole and bury yourselves early."

"This is wasteland hard currency. Blood, flesh, and the life to keep living."
