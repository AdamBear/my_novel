# Chapter 71: Jungle Black Market

## Section 1: Overdue Countdown

Black Rock City mid-level hub platform, the air filled with oppressive silence.

An hour and a half had passed since Iron Blood Mercenary Corps deputy commander "Mad Dog" promised to deliver antibiotics and surgical equipment.

In the temporary medical point at the platform corner, Su Liang leaned against a rusty iron crate, eyes tightly closed. A trace of glaring crimson slowly seeped from her right ear canal, dripping onto her gray-black tactical collar.

"Can't delay any longer."

Medic Old Ghost held a dirty gauze, anxious as an ant on a hot pan. He scanned Su Liang's head with his crude mechanical prosthetic eye, voice carrying despair. "Her auditory nerves were overdrawn too severely in the system. Now the real flesh has lost hibernation fluid protection, nerve endings are dying. Without micron-level nerve suturing equipment and anti-infection drugs, she won't just become permanently deaf—she could die from brain infection!"

Lin Yi stood not far away, using a rag to wipe that P-19 kinetic pistol seized from the processing factory.

Hearing Old Ghost's words, his gun-wiping motion stopped.

He turned his head, looking at Su Liang with closed eyes, trembling all over from pain yet not uttering a single groan. She was his most valuable "asset" on this wasteland, also the only accomplice who crossed half a century of fate with him.

Lin Yi's eyes gradually turned cold, like a storm rising over polar ice fields.

"Boss Lin!"

Refugee leader Old Zhao scrambled over from behind the sandbag cover at the platform edge, face deathly pale. "Outside... something's wrong! Those Iron Blood Mercenary Corps people didn't deliver medicine at all. Instead they pushed over several abandoned mine carts, blocking our platform's entrance and exit! Plus they mounted heavy machine guns on top!"

## Section 2: Double-Cross Calculation

"I knew it."

The Ranger boss jumped down from the off-road vehicle, mechanical left arm emitting irritable hydraulic roars. "In Black Rock City, showing wealth is suicide. You immediately offered an Ark advanced mech CPU—how could these hungry wolves be satisfied just taking it as a 'delivery fee'?"

Falcon raised his electromagnetic rifle, pulling the bolt, sneering. "They see we only have a few guns but carry three hundred combat-ineffective burdens. In their eyes, we're a piece of fat meat. Since they can kill us all, not only can they pocket the deposit, but also seize the other CPU, plus capture these three hundred to sell as slaves. Why would they bother going to Water Gang territory to requisition surgical equipment for us?"

This was pure wasteland jungle law.

No contract spirit, only force deterrence. When your force appears insufficient to defend your wealth, transactions automatically become robbery.

"They think they've got me."

Lin Yi holstered the cleaned kinetic pistol at his outer thigh, then reached into the nearby ammo crate, grabbing two AK magazines loaded with steel-core armor-piercing rounds, stuffing them into his tactical vest.

"I was still worried that opening a massacre right after arriving on someone else's turf might seem impolite."

Lin Yi rolled his neck. The [Nirvana] gene second stage's terrifying power, following his movements, emitted "crack crack" explosive sounds deep in his bones.

"Now it's perfect. They gave me the most perfect reason."

Lin Yi walked to the off-road vehicle, looking at the Ranger boss. "Lead the way. To their lair."

"Just us two?" The boss froze, glancing at the blockade line's forces outside. "They have at least a hundred fully armed mercenaries at this outpost. That's a proper gang."

"Falcon stays here, manning the heavy machine gun to hold the line. Anyone who dares charge in, blast them to pulp." Lin Yi's voice was flat as arranging an ordinary shopping trip. "We two go 'expedite delivery.'"

## Section 3: Black Water Bar

Fifteen minutes later.

Crossing several metal catwalks filled with sulfur smell and coal smoke, Lin Yi and the Ranger boss arrived at the mid-level area's most prosperous district.

Here stood a building pieced together from two massive old-era subway cars—[Black Water Bar]. This was not only Iron Blood Mercenary Corps' mid-level headquarters, but also all of Black Rock City's most notorious stolen goods black market.

Outside the bar stood four mercenaries holding rifles, boredly smoking inferior tobacco.

Lin Yi ignored them, walking straight toward the entrance.

"Stop! Iron Blood's reserved today, unauthorized personnel..." A mercenary reached out to push Lin Yi away.

Bang!

Lin Yi didn't slow down at all. He didn't even use his hands, just slightly dropped his shoulder, using forward momentum to viciously ram into that mercenary's chest.

The [Nirvana] second stage's abnormal bone density made this ram's power comparable to a full-speed charging bull. That mercenary didn't even scream—his sternum directly caved in, the whole person flying backward like a broken sack, smashing through the bar's wooden door, heavily crashing onto a table inside!

Crash!

The originally noisy Black Water Bar instantly fell silent.

The inferior heavy metal music abruptly stopped. Dozens of mercenaries inside drinking, gambling, showing off spoils uniformly stood up. Dozens of various firearms instantly leveled, black gun barrels all aimed at the entrance.

Lin Yi stepped on broken boards, expressionlessly walking into the bar.

The Ranger boss followed behind holding that large-caliber revolver. Watching this tense standoff, a trace of cold sweat appeared on his forehead, but his mechanical arm remained steady as a mountain.

In the deepest booth of the bar, that one-eyed deputy commander "Mad Dog" was embracing a scantily clad woman, the table displaying several bottles of old-era liquor.

Among those bottles, prominently placed was that [Ark Spider Mech CPU] Lin Yi gave him. It flickered with ghostly blue light, becoming Mad Dog's capital for showing off to subordinates.

## Section 4: Physical Negotiation

Seeing Lin Yi walk in, Mad Dog first froze, then pushed away the woman in his arms, erupting in extremely arrogant laughter.

"Hahahaha! Brothers, look who this is? This is that 'God of Wealth' who delivered himself!"

Mad Dog picked up the CPU from the table, tossing it in his hand, single eye full of mockery. "What? Can't wait? Didn't I have brothers go 'stand guard' outside your platform? How'd you run out?"

Lin Yi ignored the gun barrels pointed at him around. He strode through the crowd, walking straight to Mad Dog's booth.

The distance between them, only separated by a tin table.

"Where are the antibiotics and micron-level nerve suturing equipment I wanted?" Lin Yi looked at him coldly.

"Equipment? What equipment?"

Mad Dog picked his ear, putting on a rogue face. "Kid, this chip of yours is only enough to pay protection fees for your three hundred 'meat pigs' at that site. As for those advanced medical equipment you want..."

Mad Dog suddenly lowered his body, eyes becoming extremely greedy and vicious. "I heard you have another identical chip? Hand it over. Also, send over that pretty cold chick beside you to let the brothers have fun for a few days. I might consider lending you two bottles of expired anti-inflammatory drugs."

"Click click click!"

Following Mad Dog's words, over a dozen surrounding mercenaries directly pulled bolts, tightly surrounding Lin Yi and the boss in the middle.

"In this Black Rock City, dragons must coil, tigers must crouch. Understand, little punk?" Mad Dog played with the knife in his hand, fearless.

"Understood."

Lin Yi nodded slightly.

The next second.

Without drawing his gun, without extra words.

Lin Yi's left hand bearing the black brand suddenly shot out, speed so fast it left an afterimage in the air! He grabbed Mad Dog's arrogant bald head, carrying the [Nirvana] gene's terrifying explosive power, viciously, unreservedly smashing his head toward that hard tin table!

Clang—!!!

A scalp-numbing loud sound.

The heavy tin table was directly smashed into a deep crater by this terrifying force. Mad Dog's nasal bone instantly shattered, blood mixed with broken teeth spraying radially across the table surface.

"You're fucking right."

Lin Yi pressed down hard on Mad Dog's still-twitching head, raising his head. Those pitch-black eyes burned with asura-like violent killing intent, surveying the surrounding completely stunned mercenaries.

"On the wasteland, indeed shouldn't talk reason."

"Fire!!!" A mercenary roared hoarsely.

Lin Yi's right hand had already gripped the P-19 kinetic pistol's handle at his waist.

The tyrant's physical negotiation officially began.
