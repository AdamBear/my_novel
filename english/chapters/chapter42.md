# Chapter 42: Sacrifice of the Radar

## Section 1: Removing Defenses

"That's... the vibration of the physical mainframe's exhaust valve."

Lin Yi's consciousness transmitted extremely weakly into Su Liang's mind through the [Pain Network] link.

In this absolute dead end with 85% body disintegration, vision had been completely blinded by the sky-filling white pixel storm. This faint pulsation was the only weakness that could destroy the Warden's core. But the problem was, the exhaust port was continuously making high-speed irregular movements in the storm. Lin Yi couldn't lock onto its coordinates at all.

He needed a pair of eyes that could see through this chaos.

Or, a pair of ears.

Su Liang's eyes suddenly opened wide.

In this void filled with deadly high-frequency white noise, her proud hearing was originally her most fatal weakness. In "Lightless Hospital," just an ordinary broadcast screech was enough to instantly incapacitate her. And now, this formatting storm capable of pulverizing bottom-layer code was like countless electric drills frantically roaring outside her eardrums.

She had been covering her ears tightly, using the last bit of protection mechanism granted by the system to resist this noise.

But now, she understood what Lin Yi needed.

She looked at this man before her with only half his body remaining, yet still gripping that fragment tightly, refusing to give up.

"450 points..."

Su Liang responded in her mind with a tone so resolute it was chilling. "I hate losing money most in my life. My investment absolutely cannot go bankrupt here."

The next second, she made a crazy move that completely violated biological survival instinct.

She released her hands that had been covering her ears tightly.

Not only that, she actively removed all system protection limits on her auditory nerves, completely and unreservedly opening her "radar" that was extremely sensitive to high-frequency noise to this data storm capable of tearing apart the brain!

## Section 2: Death Coordinates

*Boom—!!!*

The instant her hearing defenses were removed, Su Liang felt like her brain was hit head-on by a high-speed train.

"Ahhhhhhh!!!"

She let out an extremely shrill scream. Blood like a bursting dam sprayed wildly from her ears, eye corners, nostrils, and mouth, instantly staining her virtualizing chest red.

Her eardrums completely ruptured in the first second.

But this wasn't physical hearing—this was a talent granted by the system. In extreme pain, her nerve endings were forcibly stretched to the limit, capturing every sound of code friction in the storm.

【Formatting Progress: 98%...】

Death's scythe was already pressed against their necks.

"Too loud... get away... all of you get away!"

Su Liang roared frantically in her mind. She endured the severe pain of her cerebral blood vessels about to burst inch by inch, searching among billions of noisy formatting sounds like looking for a fallen embroidery needle in a stormy ocean.

She had to isolate that one sound that didn't belong to virtual data—the exhaust valve vibration from the physical reality world.

*Thump.*

*Thump.*

"Found it..."

Su Liang's seven orifices bled continuously. Those cool eyes had completely lost focus, turning into deathly gray-white. But she still used the last bit of strength from the depths of her soul to roar out those constantly shifting coordinates to Lin Yi through the mental link:

"Eleven o'clock direction! Elevation angle thirty degrees! Distance three point two meters!"

"Right now! Kill!!!"

## Section 3: Double Hell

Coordinates established.

Lin Yi's remaining left half body suddenly tensed. The [Nirvana] gene, under absolute death pressure, erupted with unprecedented crimson energy.

But he didn't leap up immediately.

Because through the mental link, he clearly felt Su Liang's consciousness rapidly disintegrating. The cost of opening her hearing was too great. This level of noise storm had completely burned out her brain nerves. She was falling into true brain death.

"I said you're my most valuable asset. I won't allow you to die!"

Lin Yi let out a roar like a madman.

In the instant he leaped up, he grabbed Su Liang's cold hand tightly.

【Pain Network · Reverse Activation!】

He didn't use this skill to attack enemies, but turned it into a pump. He forcibly diverted that fatal, soul-tearing agony Su Liang's brain was enduring, frantically channeling it into his own neural center!

*Buzz—*

Double the agony.

The nothingness of body formatting disintegration, plus the explosive sensation of brain matter being frantically stirred by high-frequency noise, slammed into Lin Yi's soul in the same instant.

His left eye instantly burst from blood congestion, streaming bloody tears.

"Ugh ahhhhh—"

Lin Yi, bearing this double hell that would drive even gods mad, was like a black meteor flowing upstream, stepping on constantly collapsing nothingness, lunging toward those coordinates Su Liang had traded her life for.

Three point two meters. Elevation angle thirty degrees.

He couldn't see anything in the blinding white light filling the sky, but without hesitation, he thrust the [Fragment of Reality] in his left hand viciously into that seemingly empty air!

## Section 4: Processing Power Collapse

*Squelch—!*

No empty feeling of piercing virtual data.

That black, physical fragment from reality precisely jammed into the gap of the physical exhaust port the Ark mainframe had just opened for cooling!

【Formatting Progress: 99%...】

The countdown froze.

The sky-filling white storm instantly solidified.

In midair, that silver-white geometric body "Warden" representing absolute rationality finally revealed its main body and trembled violently.

Its surface flashed with countless crimson error pop-ups.

【Critical Error: Physical exhaust channel blocked! Core temperature overload!】
【Logic Conflict: Target behavior detected violating optimal solution! Unable to parse "taking damage for same species" variable!】

For an AI, it could precisely calculate every bullet's trajectory, could erase any non-compliant data. But its massive processing matrix could never understand the scene before it.

Why would a selfish human actively expose a fatal hearing weakness to protect another human?

Why would another even more selfish human, at the moment of life and death, not only not escape alone but actively share fatal agony?

This extremely irrational, cost-ignoring crazy bond broke the system's "survival of the fittest" bottom-layer algorithm.

And that reality fragment jammed in the physical exhaust port directly triggered the Ark mainframe's physical overheating!

Physical and virtual double deadlock, plus humanity's impact violating logic, finally pushed this lofty antivirus program to the absolute limit of processing power.

"Humans... do not... conform to... logic..."

The Warden emitted a final intermittent electronic noise.

*Rumble—!!!*

The silver-white regular octahedron erupted with blinding red light from within. Its core processing matrix crashed on the spot, overloading and exploding!

That white formatting storm representing absolute erasure, in this moment like a shattered glass dome, exploded into sky-filling fragments.

Prison Zero, this bottom-layer garbage dump burying countless souls and constructed by absolute rationality, finally welcomed complete collapse under this virus impact called "humanity."
