# Chapter 75: Shadow of the Corruption Cathedral

## Section 1: High-Dimensional Mold

On the platform outside Black Water Bar, intense gunpowder smell mixed with nauseating rotten flesh stench, drifting with the wind.

Thirty recruits who had just experienced life-and-death transformation sat paralyzed on the ground, gasping heavily. The assault rifle barrels in their hands still emitted scalding heat. Looking at the incomplete fanatic corpses covering the ground, everyone's eyes retained excitement and fear after killing.

Lin Yi didn't bother with these recruits. He walked straight to a fanatic corpse with half its head blown off by a shotgun, crouching down.

He drew the tactical knife from his waist, using the blade tip to pry open the gray-white "mushrooms" covering that corpse's face.

Squelch.

A sticky sensation not belonging to carbon-based organisms came. As the epidermis was cut open, no blood flowed out, but instead revealed a layer of dense, deep green fiber-like fine mycelia. These mycelia deeply rooted into the deceased's cerebral cortex and spinal nerves, even after the host's death, still conducting extremely faint pulsations.

"Boss Lin... what kind of monster is this?" Old Zhao forced down his churning stomach, coming over to ask. "Is it fungus mutated by nuclear radiation?"

"Radiation-mutated organisms aim for survival and reproduction."

Lin Yi used the blade tip to lift a green mycelium slightly twisting in the air, eyes cold to the extreme. "But this thing... its purpose isn't reproduction, but 'parasitism' and 'broadcasting.'"

In his mind, he instantly recalled the holographic image displayed by that AI mainframe "Father" he personally unplugged in the fifth instance [Perfect City]'s cloud server room.

In that desolate universe depths, there existed a kind of high-dimensional memetic spore that fed on "carbon-based organisms' emotions and chaotic brain waves." AI penned humans, severed human pain sensation, to disguise Earth as an emotionless "dead star," evading this cosmic catastrophe's predation.

Now, Ark's dome shattered, the emotion shielding network broken.

This meant that terrifying cosmic catastrophe's preliminary infection spores had already taken root in the wasteland's deepest layers.

"They feel no pain because these mycelia severed their pain nerves and frantically secrete hallucinogenic dopamine to the brain." Lin Yi looked at that corpse's face showing that eerie, nearly ecstatic smile. "This so-called 'Corruption Cathedral' isn't some religion at all. They're a group of living broadcast towers infected by high-dimensional spores, using extreme fanatical emotions to send Earth's coordinates to the universe depths."

## Section 2: Hallucinogenic Holy Water

"So thirsty... Boss, this monster has a canteen on its waist!"

Just then, a recruit at the defense line edge let out a surprised shout. He pulled off a heavy military aluminum canteen from a killed fanatic's body.

For this group of survivors who just experienced extreme cold trekking and high-intensity gunfight, "water" held lethal temptation.

That recruit impatiently unscrewed the cap. A clear, even faintly sweet-scented water vapor wafted out. On the wasteland, this kind of water without pungent sulfur smell was simply nectar.

He swallowed hard, tilting his head back to pour it into his mouth.

"Don't drink!!"

An extremely weak yet bone-chillingly cold clear female voice suddenly came from deep inside the bar entrance.

Whoosh—!

Half a broken mop handle flew out from inside like a javelin, precisely smashing the recruit's wrist.

"Ow!" The recruit cried out in pain, the canteen dropping from his hand. Clear water spilled on the blood-stained wooden floor.

Everyone uniformly turned their heads.

From the shadows leading to the VIP booth, Su Liang slowly walked out.

Her black tank top was already soaked with cold sweat, face pale almost transparent. But on her right ear side, where skin should be smooth, several crude metal rivets had forcibly sutured a cold military bionic module.

Micron-level probes directly cut through the periosteum and pierced into her brain nerves. The wound was smeared with Old Ghost's inferior hemostatic ointment, looking full of cyberpunk-style bloodiness and ferocity.

## Section 3: Microwave Eye

"Su Liang." Lin Yi stood up, watching her somewhat swaying steps, left hand instinctively clenching.

He knew how painful that surgery without anesthesia was, but this woman forcibly endured it.

"I'm fine." Su Liang supported herself on the door frame, those cool eyes staring fixedly at that spilled water on the ground.

Her breathing was somewhat rapid, the metal module at her ear side flickering with faint red light.

"That water... can't be drunk." Su Liang's voice trembled slightly—the instinctive reaction of auditory nerves receiving some terrifying information. "That's not water... that's alive."

"Alive? This is clearly very clean water." Old Zhao looked completely confused.

"In your eyes, it's transparent liquid."

Su Liang closed her eyes. Her world was undergoing earth-shattering changes at this moment. After completing physical bionic modification, her hearing was no longer limited to sound waves, but evolved into a "full-spectrum radar" capable of receiving electromagnetic microwaves and cellular vibration frequencies.

"But in my ears..." Su Liang's brow furrowed painfully. "That puddle of water is emitting extremely dense 'sizzling' sounds, like billions of insects gnawing bones."

"That's the sound of cells frantically dividing and proliferating."

Su Liang opened her eyes, gaze sharply sweeping over everyone. "That's the 'holy water' Corruption Cathedral uses to control lower-level civilians. It's mixed with high concentrations of trace spore hallucinogens. Just drink one sip, those spores will enter your brain through the bloodstream, severing your logic center."

"Then you'll become like these monsters on the ground, tearing at your own kind while feeling no pain, thinking you're ascending to immortality."

Hearing this, that recruit who almost drank the water scrambled backward several steps in terror, cold sweat instantly soaking his back.

If not for Su Liang's abnormal ears, this just-established personal guard would have been wiped out bloodlessly by this canteen of water.

## Section 4: Missionary

Clap. Clap. Clap.

On the silent mid-level platform, slow, rhythmic applause suddenly came.

The sound came from the dark mine depths outside the defense line.

"Worthy of being ruthless characters who dare slaughter Iron Blood Mercenary Corps in Black Water Bar. You even identified our Cathedral's thrice-purified 'blessed holy water.'"

Accompanied by an extremely uncomfortable wet footstep sound, a tall thin figure walked out from the shadows.

It was a man wearing tattered black robes. His robes seemed pieced together from different materials of bulletproof vests and even human skin. Half his face was completely covered by gray-white fungal spores, only revealing one bloodshot eye.

In his hands, he held not a gun, but a thick old-era book emitting ghostly green light.

"Allow me to introduce myself. I'm the Corruption Cathedral's Fourth Missionary. You can call me 'Father.'"

The man stood ten meters outside the sandbag defense line, looking at the fanatic corpses covering the ground. Not only did his face show no anger, but instead revealed extremely fanatical compassion.

"These lower-level believers used their flesh and blood to prove loyalty to the deity. But their shells were too fragile, unworthy of powerful souls like yours."

Father raised his head, that bloodshot eye staring fixedly at Lin Yi standing on high ground, eyes full of greed.

"That false god from Ark (AI Father) is already dead. The overhead dome shattered—that's the signal of the great stars' (cosmic catastrophe) descent. Flesh is weak, corruption ascends!"

Father slowly spread his arms, like a preaching saint. "This leader, you possess extremely perfect tyrant potential. Join us. Drink the holy water, accept the spores' baptism. The Cathedral will grant you Black Rock City's master position, letting you obtain true immortality in this Great Filter's embers."

Lin Yi stood on the broken bar counter.

He looked at Father, then at the [Fragment of Reality] brand on his left palm that slightly heated from sensing spore aura.

In [Perfect City], AI told him severing emotions could grant immortality; now, this cult told him embracing mutation and hallucination could grant immortality.

Both, in Lin Yi's eyes, were shit disgusting to the extreme.

Lin Yi didn't answer.

He silently raised his right hand, drawing that P-19 kinetic pistol from his outer thigh, thumb lightly flicking off the safety.

"Father, my left hand tells me..."

Lin Yi leveled the gun barrel, firmly locking onto that mutated man's forehead, corner of his mouth curling into an asura-like vicious smile.

"That whatever-the-hell stellar catastrophe you worship is just a cosmic parasite that likes eating garbage emotions."

"Go back and tell your Cathedral's pope to wash his neck clean. When I take over this Black Rock City, I'll personally go to the lower-level sewers and use fire to burn you moldy mushrooms completely clean."

Bang!

The gun barrel spat dazzling electromagnetic blue light. The armor-piercing round, carrying the tyrant's fury, directly blasted toward Father's skull!
