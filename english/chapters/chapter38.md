# Chapter 38: A Single Spark Can Start a Prairie Fire

## Section 1: The Craziest Script

"You want to touch those tens of thousands of lunatics?"

In the junction chamber, Sample 01's hoarse static voice almost changed pitch, revealing barely concealed shock. "Are you insane?! Those are all defective products tortured by the system until their logic collapsed! Even if you only receive one-thousandth of their pain, your pitiful carbon-based brain will explode like an egg in a microwave!"

"This is the only way."

Lin Yi leaned against the metal duct wall, coldly looking at his left hand with the [Fragment of Reality]. "The Warden is an absolutely rational antivirus program. Physical attacks are useless against it because it can modify gravity and space at will. Our only chance is to burst its memory with something it can't parse."

"What thing?" Su Liang frowned.

"Irrational 'humanity.'" Lin Yi raised his head, his eyes showing a chilling fervor. "I'm going to turn all the 'garbage' in this prison back into 'people.'"

Without giving 01 or Su Liang a chance to object, Lin Yi suddenly kicked open the louvers on the other side of the ventilation duct.

Below was Prison Zero's most massive central containment area. Countless suspended cages were arranged like a honeycomb, and on the plaza at the bottom, tens of thousands of mindless monsters wandered densely. They bit and devoured each other, repeating meaningless slaughter in an endless hell.

"Help me watch the Warden's position and call out its location."

Lin Yi turned to Su Liang, left these words, and without any hesitation, leaped into that dark abyss.

*Whoosh—*

Wind howled in his ears. Lin Yi crashed heavily onto the cold metal grid of the containment area plaza. The massive impact made his throat sweet, but he forcibly swallowed that mouthful of blood.

His appearance was like throwing fresh meat into a calm crocodile pool.

The movements of hundreds of surrounding monsters instantly stopped. Their turbid, red-glowing eyes all locked onto this suddenly arrived "normal human."

*Roar—!!!*

The nearest half-mechanical zombie let out a roar, waving its arm equipped with a rusty chainsaw, charging at Lin Yi like an out-of-control tank.

## Section 2: Ten Thousand Ghosts Devouring the Heart

Lin Yi didn't run. He didn't even assume a defensive posture.

He stared intently at the charging mechanical zombie. Just as the chainsaw was about to split his face, he suddenly stepped forward, his left hand advancing rather than retreating, pressing onto the zombie's half-rotten forehead!

【Pain Network · Forced Assimilation!】

*Buzz—!!!*

The moment his fingertips made contact, the [Fragment of Reality] pierced deep into the zombie's flesh. A massive, desperate memory fragment with a strong bloody smell blasted directly into Lin Yi's mind like a high-pressure water gun.

It was a veteran who voluntarily stayed behind to cover his teammates' retreat. He was bitten to pieces alive in an endless sea of corpses, but when the system recycled his consciousness, all he could think about was "Did my brothers escape?"

"Ugh ahhh—"

Lin Yi's knees buckled and he fell to the ground. That pain of having his body torn apart, that despair of a hero's end, instantly occupied his neural center. Two streams of nosebleed dripped down his chin.

But he endured it.

"You're not garbage..." Lin Yi gripped the zombie's head tightly, his eyes bloodshot, his voice hoarse as if dripping blood. "You're a hero... Remember! Open your eyes and look at yourself!"

A miracle happened.

The originally berserk mechanical zombie's raised chainsaw froze in midair. In its turbid single eye, a trace of extremely human pain and confusion flashed. It looked at its own rusty mechanical arm, and from its throat came a shrill, human-like wail of grief.

It stepped back two paces, and instead of attacking Lin Yi again, it turned to stand in front of him, facing the other surging monsters.

"One..."

Lin Yi gasped heavily, climbing up from the ground. He didn't stop, but like a crazed beast, actively charged into the corpse tide.

He extended his left hand covered with blue veins and grabbed a deformed monster pieced together from rotting flesh.

Memories flooded in. It was the despair of a girl who was betrayed by a trusted lover in an instance and pushed into an abyss.

"Take it back!" Lin Yi's eyes tore at the corners, bleeding. The deformed monster stopped moving and let out a heart-wrenching wail.

He crashed into the embrace of a massive fungal mutant.

Memories flooded in. It was the guilt of a father who voluntarily participated in trials to earn medical expenses for his sick daughter but ultimately went mad.

"Take it back! Hate this damn system!" Lin Yi vomited a mouthful of blood.

Ten, a hundred, over a thousand...

## Section 3: Cyber Psychosis

Lin Yi was like a missionary walking in darkness. In the most tragic way, using his own brain as a relay station, he forcibly stuffed those memories the system considered "redundant bugs" back into the depths of these monsters' souls.

This was an extremely cruel torture.

Lin Yi's seven orifices were bleeding. His mental points were plummeting at a terrifying speed. Every second, he had to experience the deaths, betrayals, and despair of hundreds of people.

If not for the [Nirvana] gene running frantically in extreme pain, forcibly maintaining his sanity, he would have long become a puddle of mindless rotten flesh.

"Lin Yi! The Warden is coming down!"

From the ventilation duct overhead came Su Liang's anxious, voice-breaking shout.

Lin Yi struggled to raise his head.

In the void above the containment area, that massive regular octahedron made of silver-white bottom-layer code was slowly descending with absolute order and coldness.

The Warden had detected the anomaly here. According to the system's bottom-layer logic, this large area of "data garbage" had uncontrollable logic overflow and must be completely formatted.

*Buzz—*

A silver-white light curtain over a hundred meters in diameter descended from the sky like death's guillotine. Wherever the curtain swept, whether metal floor or monsters, they were instantly reduced to two-dimensional textures, then turned into green garbled code and disappeared.

"Run! Lin Yi! Get away!" Su Liang shouted from above.

But Lin Yi couldn't move anymore. He had exhausted all his mental strength. Even standing was now a luxury. He slumped on the metal grid, watching that curtain symbolizing absolute erasure approach him, but his lips curved into an extremely manic smile.

Because he knew he didn't need to run anymore.

*Roar—!!!*

An earth-shattering roar echoed throughout Prison Zero.

It wasn't meaningless monster howling—it was thousands of souls who had regained their "humanity" issuing the angriest accusation against this system that treated human life as worthless!

## Section 4: Collapse of Logic

The half-mechanical veteran zombie awakened by Lin Yi was the first to charge out.

It knew that light curtain was erasure at the physical law level, but it still raised its rusty chainsaw and charged at the curtain without hesitation. Its massive body was flattened and deleted the instant it touched the curtain, but in the zero-point-one second before deletion, it used all its strength to hurl the chainsaw in its hand viciously at the Warden's main body!

Then came the deformed girl, the fungal father, the tens of thousands of awakened defective monsters!

They didn't flee. Like a group of true, crazed "cyber psychotics," they rushed forward one after another, using their broken data bodies to fill that curtain representing absolute death.

"Don't... hurt... him!"

Some monster, using its broken vocal cords, roared out this human language.

Then the entire containment area boiled. Tens of thousands of monsters used their flesh and blood to build a high wall above Lin Yi's head, using suicidal charges to assault that lofty geometric body!

【Warning: Large number of targets detected performing actions contrary to survival instinct.】
【Warning: Logic calculation conflict. Unable to parse "self-sacrifice" variable.】
【System processing power overload rate: 400%... 800%...】

The Warden's originally flawless silver-white surface began flashing red and green error codes frantically. Its light curtain that could instantly kill anything actually showed severe lag and delay under the assault of tens of thousands of "illogical" irrational suicidal charges!

For an AI, it could calculate bullet trajectories, calculate monster movement patterns.

But it could never calculate why a group of data considered garbage would willingly die to protect a human they just met.

"See that..."

Lin Yi, covered in blood, leaned against the iron pillar behind him. He looked up at those ugly monsters continuously vanishing in the light yet still charging forward, tears mixed with blood flowing down.

"This is humanity."

He looked at the Warden in midair, beginning to tremble and deform violently from processing overload, and let out a grin more monstrous than monsters:

"Now, who's the real garbage?"
