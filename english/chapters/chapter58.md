# Chapter 58: Wind Listener and Executioner

## Section 1: Blind Cuts in the Sandstorm

Ratatatatata—!!!

The two heavy defense mechs' Gatling guns first erupted with deafening roars. The terrifying firing rate of six thousand rounds per minute instantly wove two orange-red metal storms, brutally tearing through the overwhelming yellow sand, blasting the dune in front of the assault vehicle like boiling water.

The high-temperature bullets even instantly melted sand grains into tiny glass crystals.

"Scatter! Find cover!"

The Ranger boss roared, his thick mechanical left arm shoving the car door. His entire body rolled out from the passenger seat like a giant bear, hiding behind a protruding rock. Falcon grabbed the heavy electromagnetic rifle, rolled on the spot, and vanished into the sandstorm's shadows.

But Lin Yi didn't hide.

Facing that barrage capable of tearing an armored vehicle to shreds, his pupils held no fear—only absolute focus catalyzed to the extreme by the [Nirvana] gene.

"Su Liang!" Lin Yi crouched behind the car door, both hands leveling the old-era AK rifle, voice steady amid the gunfire.

"Eleven o'clock direction, distance twenty-five meters, three infantry, armor thickness three millimeters." Su Liang closed her eyes, back against the wheel, hands desperately covering the edges of her ears, reporting coordinates in a pained yet extremely precise cadence. "They're changing formation... the middle one is raising his gun! Three, two, one!"

Bang bang bang!

Lin Yi didn't use his eyes to aim through the sandstorm. The instant Su Liang counted to "one," he directly relied on instinct and muscle memory, pulling the trigger at that gray hazy sandstorm.

Three 7.62mm steel-core armor-piercing rounds howled out.

Three dull metallic shattering sounds came from deep in the sandstorm. Those three Ark elite guards trying to flank from the side didn't even have time to see the enemy's position before their gas masks were precisely blown off, red and white liquid mixed with machine oil spraying onto the sand.

"Two o'clock direction, five! Distance thirty meters!" Su Liang's speech rate grew faster, cold sweat seeping from her forehead. "They're using thermal imaging scans!"

"Thermal imaging doesn't work on the dead."

Lin Yi kicked open the assault vehicle's fuel tank cap, backhandedly drawing the P-19 kinetic pistol from his outer thigh, firing one shot at the fuel tank.

BOOM!

The assault vehicle's remaining fuel instantly exploded, igniting towering flames. The intense heat instantly disrupted all surrounding thermal imaging detection.

Using the firelight as cover, Lin Yi shot forward like black lightning, viciously cutting into the Ark guards' formation.

## Section 2: Joints' Lament

Thud!

Lin Yi's gun stock brutally smashed a guard's throat. Simultaneously, his left hand delivered a heavy punch, using the inhuman strength from gene awakening to directly dent another guard's chest armor.

In just thirty seconds, half of the twenty-four exoskeleton guards fell.

"Warning! Defense line damaged! Mech units execute indiscriminate fire!"

Accompanied by piercing electronic sounds, those two four-meter-tall heavy mechs finally locked onto Lin Yi's position. The thick gun barrels rotated again, dense bullet rain sweeping over like death's scythe.

"Boss! Suppressing fire on the left one!"

Lin Yi frantically weaved through the hail of bullets, roaring.

"Roger!" From behind the ruins, the Ranger boss shouldered a man-portable rocket launcher. His mechanical left arm firmly gripped the launch base, pulling the trigger.

Whoosh—BOOM!

The rocket accurately hit the left mech's shield. Though it didn't penetrate that thick composite armor, the violent explosion impact caused the mech's movements to briefly freeze.

"The right one's mine!"

Lin Yi's gaze sharpened, locking onto the right mech frantically pouring out firepower.

"Lin Yi, don't face it head-on!" Su Liang's extremely penetrating voice came through the comm channel (guard earpieces they'd seized). "This sandy ground is too soft, the mech weighs over eight tons. I hear its right leg knee joint servo motor emitting high-frequency friction sounds—sand got into its hydraulic gaps!"

"Its right leg load-bearing capacity is declining! It'll turn slower!"

This was the terror of auditory radar. In Su Liang's ears, those seemingly impeccable steel behemoths were all defective products assembled from noisy components.

"Understood."

Lin Yi holstered his rifle, suddenly drawing the tactical knife from his outer thigh. He didn't choose to retreat, but instead charged at that mech close to the ground.

The instant the mech rotated its cumbersome body, trying to aim its gun barrel at him.

Lin Yi kicked off with both legs, his entire body tracing a strange arc in midair, directly passing through the mech's firing blind spot, sliding under the mech's right leg.

"Kneel for me!"

Lin Yi roared, the tactical knife in his hand carrying desolate cold gleam, precisely stabbing into the hydraulic line gap at the mech's right knee joint worn by sand, then viciously pulling downward!

Hiss—!

High-pressure coolant and black machine oil instantly erupted.

The eight-ton mech lost its right leg support. Its massive body collapsed with ear-piercing metallic twisting sounds, the heavy Gatling gun barrel crashing heavily onto the sand, completely silenced.

## Section 3: Meat Grinder Blades

The other mech was also destroyed at its core under the boss and Falcon's heavy crossfire.

Before the exhaust port, only mechanical wreckage and burning flames remained.

But the danger wasn't resolved.

Ahead, that thirty-meter-tall black metal structure emitted deafening rumbling. Twelve giant industrial exhaust fans over five meters in diameter rotated like twelve crazed meat grinders, expelling Ark's internal waste heat skyward.

Heat waves rolled. Standing before the fans, the temperature exceeded at least sixty degrees, the air itself distorting.

"Fuck! How do we get in?"

The Ranger boss dragged his somewhat overheated mechanical arm over, looking at those massive alloy blades spinning so fast even afterimages couldn't be seen, single eye full of shock. "At this rotation speed, even an armored vehicle thrown in would be instantly ground to iron filings. We can't get through!"

"Cut the power?" Falcon asked.

"Useless. This belongs to the mainframe's physical cooling system, has independent triple backup power." Lin Yi stepped forward, feeling the scorching gale hitting his face, that tattered hospital gown wildly dancing in the wind.

In Prison Zero, he had used the fragment in his hand to jam the virtual dimension's exhaust valve, causing the Warden's overload.

Now, he would reenact it in the physical world.

"Since we can't shut it down, jam it."

Lin Yi turned to look at the heavy mech he'd just disabled on the ground, gaze locking onto its two-meter-long Gatling gun barrel forged from high-strength tungsten steel.

"Boss, help me out."

Lin Yi walked over, both hands gripping one end of the barrel.

The boss immediately understood, stepping forward, using that mechanical left arm erupting with hydraulic rumbling to firmly grasp the barrel's other end.

"On my count, stab it into the center blade's bearing!" Lin Yi gritted his teeth.

"You're crazy?! The recoil will shatter our arms!" Though the boss said this, his mechanical arm didn't loosen at all.

"One!"

"Two!"

"Three! Go!"

Both simultaneously let out hysterical roars, lifting that several-hundred-pound tungsten steel barrel like a battering ram, viciously stabbing it into that high-speed rotating giant fan blade bearing!

## Section 4: Cutting the Windpipe

CLANG—!!!!!

An extremely terrifying metallic collision sound, as if the earth itself was being torn apart, exploded in the sandstorm.

Sparks sprayed out like a waterfall.

The high-speed rotating alloy blades viciously hacked at the tungsten steel barrel. The massive recoil instantly conducted back through the barrel. The boss's mechanical left arm immediately burst with blue electric sparks, several hydraulic lines directly rupturing; Lin Yi's tiger's mouth also instantly tore, blood flowing freely.

But the tungsten steel barrel was firmly jammed in the gear gap.

Crack crack crack... BANG!

Accompanied by teeth-grinding twisting sounds, that five-meter-diameter giant fan finally emitted a lament under extreme torque. Three massive alloy blades broke off at the roots, spinning as they flew out, crashing heavily into distant dunes.

The wind stopped.

The originally airtight exhaust port now had a huge two-meter-diameter black hole.

A scalding heat wave carrying thick machine oil smell, nutrient solution's sweet fishy smell, and some decaying odor surged violently from that black hole.

"The passage is open." Lin Yi gasped heavily, shaking his blood-covered hands.

"The temperature inside is too high. We don't have heat-resistant suits—going down is entering an oven." Falcon looked at that bottomless black hole, brow furrowed.

"We must go down. Ark's mainframe is below this."

Lin Yi turned his head, looking at Su Liang. "Can you do it?"

Su Liang's face was pale, but she wasted no words, backhandedly inserting her knife into the thigh sheath, walking to the exhaust port. Those cool eyes showed a fierceness of facing death without regret.

"The interest isn't settled yet, of course I'm following the creditor."

Lin Yi smiled.

He hesitated no more, leaping forward, directly jumping into that scalding, dark abyss. Immediately after, Su Liang, the boss, and Falcon also jumped down one after another.

They were like several poisonous sharp blades, following Ark's most vulnerable respiratory tract, viciously cutting toward this steel behemoth's heart.
