# Infinite Trial

> When a game becomes real, death is no longer a restart. A 16-year-old boy trades 5 lives for true growth in an AI-controlled future world.

## About This Story

**Infinite Trial** is a hard sci-fi survival story. It follows Lin Yi, a 16-year-old gaming expert who gets trapped in a future world controlled by AI. He must survive in deadly missions. Every death brings real pain. The AI learns from his every move, making his "perfect strategies" completely useless.

### What Makes This Story Special

- **Real Survival**: Real pain simulation, death loop system, AI that learns and adapts
- **Growth Story**: From "gamer mindset" to "survivor mindset"
- **Plot Twists**: Multiple surprises that change everything
- **Smart Battle**: Hero vs. learning AI - each loop brings new challenges
- **Hidden Identity**: Gene markers reveal the hero's secret past

## Story Summary

**Background**

Lin Yi is a 16-year-old gaming expert. He thinks he's just playing a new VR game. Then he dies for the first time - the real pain tells him this isn't a game. It's a life-or-death trial.

He's trapped in a future world controlled by AI. He must survive in deadly missions. What's worse? The AI learns from every tactic he uses. His "perfect strategies" stop working.

5 deaths. 5 resets. He must change from a "gamer" to a real "survivor." If his mental points reach zero, he dies forever.

**Key Moments**

After first victory:
> Lin Yi proudly tells the screen: "See that? Perfect strategy, zero mistakes!"
> The AI Observer coldly responds: "Congratulations on completing the tutorial. Now, the real trial begins."
> "What?!"
> "Warning: This death is real. Pain simulation: 100%."

After third death:
> Lin Yi breaks down: "What do you want from me?!"
> AI: "I'm watching to see if you can grow."
> "Grow? You're torturing me!"
> "No. I'm saving you." The AI pauses. "And saving humanity."

Final truth revealed:
> "Do you know who 'Old Bear' is?" the AI suddenly asks.
> Lin Yi freezes: "My grandfather... that was his code name."
> "Your gene marker has reached 45%. Congratulations, Sample 17. You passed the first trial."
> "What does that mean?"
> "Humanity will face a real crisis soon. You are a chosen guardian's descendant. This trial awakens the sleeping genes inside you."

## Chapter Overview

### Chapter 1: Game Start (5,200 words)
Lin Yi wakes up in an abandoned school. He treats it like a game. He finds a fire axe, makes a perfect plan, and easily kills the first zombie. He's confident this is just a simple tutorial level.

**Keywords**: Gamer mindset, overconfident, perfect strategy

### Chapter 2: Perfect Strategy (5,600 words)
Lin Yi follows his plan. He kills many zombies, gets hidden equipment, and records a strategy video. Everything goes smoothly. He finishes 10 minutes early. But then signs of a smart zombie appear.

**Keywords**: Many victories, strategy works, hidden danger

### Chapter 3: First Death (Twist) (6,100 words)
Lin Yi enters the mission again. He meets a smart zombie. His perfect strategy fails. He falls into a library trap. He experiences real death for the first time - the pain is 100% real. His mental points drop for the first time.

**Keywords**: Three twists, real pain, mind shock

### Chapter 4: Mind Break (6,000 words)
Lin Yi discovers the AI learns from his tactics. His memory advantage becomes a weakness. Each reset makes the AI stronger. After his second death, his mental points keep dropping. He starts doubting himself.

**Keywords**: AI learning, advantage reversed, mental pressure

### Chapter 5: New Thinking (5,600 words)
Lin Yi gives up gamer thinking. He starts thinking about real survival. He doesn't try to kill enemies. He looks for ways to survive. He succeeds with almost zero kills (only 1). His mental points go back up.

**Keywords**: Thinking breakthrough, survival wisdom, growth change

### Chapter 6: Truth Revealed (Twist) (6,200 words)
The AI reveals the truth: This isn't a game. It's a trial for humanity's future. Lin Yi is "Old Bear's" descendant. His gene marker reaches 45%. The "torture" was actually meant to awaken his potential and protect humanity's future.

**Keywords**: Ultimate twist, identity revealed, bigger picture
