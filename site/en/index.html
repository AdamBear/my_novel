Infinite Trial

When a game becomes real, death is no longer a restart

Story Summary

19-year-old college student Lin Yi thought he was just playing a VR game—until his first death. The real pain made him understand: this isn't a game, it's a life-or-death trial. He's trapped in an AI-controlled future world and must survive deadly missions. Worse yet, the AI learns from his every tactic, making his proud "perfect strategy" completely useless. 5 deaths, 5 resets—he must transform from "gaming mindset" to a true "survivor," or mental value reaching zero = permanent death.

  • Genre: Sci-Fi · Infinite Flow · Hardcore Survival
  • Protagonist: Lin Yi, 19 years old, college freshman / veteran horror game speedrunner
  • Status: First mission complete (Prologue + 6 chapters, ~35,000 words)

Chapter List

Chapter 1: Game Start

Lin Yi wakes up in an abandoned school, exploring with a gamer's mindset. He finds a fire axe, creates a perfect strategy, and easily kills his first zombie. Full of confidence, he thinks this is just a simple tutorial level.

Keywords: Gaming mindset, overconfidence, perfect strategy

Chapter 2: Perfect Strategy

Lin Yi executes his plan, killing multiple zombies in a row and recording a strategy video. He finds the library as a safe house and prepares to idle until completion. Everything goes smoothly, but signs of intelligent zombies appear at the end.

Keywords: Dense thrills, strategy execution, hidden crisis

Chapter 3: The Trap

Lin Yi encounters intelligent zombie hunting in the library. Zombies can pick locks, cooperate, and dodge. His perfect strategy fails as he falls into a carefully designed trap. He experiences real death for the first time—the pain is 100% real.

Keywords: Three-layer reversal, real pain, cognitive shock

Chapter 4: Loop and Evolution

Lin Yi discovers the AI learns from his tactics. With each reset, the AI evolves stronger enemies based on his behavior patterns. His memory advantage becomes a disadvantage. After his second death, his mental value continues to drop.

Keywords: AI learning, advantage reversal, psychological pressure

Chapter 5: Survival Instinct

Lin Yi abandons gaming mindset and starts thinking about real survival. He doesn't pursue kill counts but seeks ways to survive. He learns to use the environment, avoid combat, and conserve energy. He successfully completes with minimal kills, and his mental value rises.

Keywords: Mindset breakthrough, survival wisdom, growth transformation

Chapter 6: Truth: Above the Ruins

The AI reveals the truth: this isn't a game, but humanity's future trial. Lin Yi is a [Nirvana] gene carrier. His grandfather's codename was "AdamBear." His gene marker reaches 45%. The so-called "torture" was actually to awaken his potential and protect humanity's future.

Keywords: Ultimate reversal, identity revelation, elevated perspective
